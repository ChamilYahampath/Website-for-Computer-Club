Our Journey

The journey of University Of Kelaniya Computer Club (UKCC) started on 13th September 2012 with authorization from the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) of University Of Kelaniya. Realizing the key importance to adapt along with upgrading technology, six chief founding members introduced this club with its mission and vision all set. The goal was to build up a strong community of UKCC students by offering them a potential platform that deals with technology and its advancement. University Of Kelaniya Computer Club holds a high status as one of the most inspiring and admiring clubs in University.
