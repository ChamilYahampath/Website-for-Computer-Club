ICT Awareness and Seminar Program on Cyber Security

On 21st June, 2023, a seminar titled “ICT Awareness and Seminar Program on Cyber Security” was organized by ISPAB & bpc in collaboration with University of Kelaniya Computer Club (UKCC). The seminar was chaired by Mohammad A Kayum Rashed, Joint Secretary General, ISPAB. The main objective of the seminar was to share forefront knowledge and information by cybersecurity experts to secure communication in navigating the digital landscape safely. The seminar began at 12:30 PM at Multipurpose Hall.

UKCC Cyber Gaming Fest 2023

University of Kelaniya Computer Club (UKCC) hosted the fourth “UKCC Cyber Gaming Fest 2023” from 1st to 4th April 2023 at University of Kelaniya. This event was sponsored by GIGABYTE, AORUS, CLUB G1 IT, THE DAILY JUGANTOR, THE BUSINESS STANDARD, EKATTOR TV, and ICE TODAY (UKCC). As one of the biggest Cyber Gaming Fests of this year, this year’s event featured FIFA 22, Need for Speed: Most Wanted, DOTA 2, Valorant, PES, and Mobile legends: bang bang.
